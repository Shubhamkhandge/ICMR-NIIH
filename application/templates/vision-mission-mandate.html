Welcome to Indian Council of Medical Research - National Institute of Immunohaematology (ICMR-NIIH)

Indian Council of Medical Research - National Institute of Immunohaematology (ICMR-NIIH), a pioneering research institute dedicated to the advancement of hematology, transfusion medicine, and immunology. As a leading center of excellence under the umbrella of ICMR, our focus is on conducting groundbreaking research in both basic and translational sciences that impact human health on a global scale.

Our Vision

The vision of ICMR-National Institute of Immunohaematology (ICMR-NIIH) is to be a globally recognized center of excellence for research in the fields of hematology, transfusion medicine, immunology, and related disciplines. We aim to bridge the gap between basic science and clinical applications, ensuring the development of innovative diagnostic and therapeutic strategies to improve the management and prevention of hematological and immunological disorders. Our goal is to contribute significantly to advancing public health and improving the quality of life for affected individuals through research, education, and community outreach.

Our Mission

Generate and Share Knowledge: Conduct research and share findings to improve clinical practices and develop new therapies.

Focus on Vulnerable Populations: Address health challenges of marginalized communities and ensure equitable access to care.

Leverage Modern Technologies: Use advanced tools to improve understanding and management of diseases.

Promote Innovation in Healthcare: Drive advancements in diagnostics, treatments, and prevention methods.

Foster Research Culture: Strengthen research in academic and health institutions.

Our Mandate

1
Research Excellence: Conduct high-impact research in hematology, immunology, and transfusion medicine to develop novel therapies for inherited and acquired conditions.
2
Clinical Integration: Collaborate with hospitals and institutions to translate research into real-world health solutions.
3
Education and Training: Offer M.Sc., Ph.D., and short-term programs to nurture young talent in life sciences and promote academic excellence.
4
Public Health and Community Services: Provide diagnostic services, genetic counseling, and prenatal diagnosis for families affected by hematological and immunological disorders.
5
International Collaboration: Build global partnerships with institutions like IIT-B, ICT-Mumbai, and NIAID-NIH for joint research and knowledge exchange.
